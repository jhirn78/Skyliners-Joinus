We here at Skyliners want to expand our amazing team, and we think you would be a great Candidate! We currently are in need of three positions on our team.

  • Feild Representative: This position is for the adventurous, the daring, and those that aren't afraid to spend some time away from home. Our business and community is growing, and we want members that can get outside and take some amazing pictures of our own. To join, you must already be a skilled and professional photographer that is ready to start shooting right away. 
  • Social media manager: As the social media manager, you are able to work from the comfort of your own home. You are in charge of creating posts to promote the account, storing Direct Messages for later use, and setting up virtual meetings with the rest of the team to pick a weekly competition winner. 
  • Photo editor: For our promotional pictures, we are in need of a professional photo editor to make sure our photos look the best they can. This is another position available to work from home, but there will be a monthly online meeting with the company executives to discuss the work done. 
Working with us has many positives, and qualities you will not find anywhere else. 

 

  1. Our team is very tight knit, and a true bond is created every time we work together.
  2. Our team is very young, and the idea grew up with the team itself, meaning that ideas are coherent, and there is not a thinking gap between editors and executives. 
  3. We provide funds for travel and living costs for our members that need to go from place to place for shoots. 
  4. Many positions are available to work at home, with the exception of the yearly in-person team meeting. 
  5. Our business revolves around the creativity of ourselves and others, which means that new ideas and thoughts are not only accepted but needed for our success. 

 

If you take a fancy to these qualities, we would love to have you as a part of our team, and hope that we will meet you very soon!
